PongAbsolute

My fourth project was a simple 2D Pong game which I made with a classmate.

This project was made using python, pyCharm and some libraries.

In this game you need to move your padle in front of the ball moving your own hand.

This game tracks your hand using an webcam.
You can play this game alone or with an friend.
This game was suposed to be something that could be placed in the schools lobby for the students
to play and get some movement while they do that.
